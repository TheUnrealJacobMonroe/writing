## Strengths

1. **The Opening Dialogue is excellent.** The friendship example is the right level of specific — concrete enough to land, universal enough to resonate. The final exchange ("Free will is the freedom to be a better cause") is a strong closer for the dialogue section.

2. **The cautionary paragraph is crucial.** The section addressing "this does not mean every bad thing that happens to you is your fault" shows editorial maturity. Without it, the principle could read as victim-blaming. The distinction between individual and collective causation is well drawn.

3. **The Alchemical Working is practical.** Five steps, no special equipment, takes 10-15 minutes. The "liberating question" framing ("What thought could I hold instead?") avoids toxic positivity by explicitly saying "Not a thought that denies reality."

4. **The transition note lands.** It doesn't just say "Book Two is coming." It makes the reader sit with what they've accomplished ("Let yourself feel the weight of it") before pointing forward. The key metaphor ("A key in your pocket opens no doors") is clean.

5. **The apprenticeship tracker is well-designed.** Three questions, one effect per evening, seven days. The end-of-week review prompt ("The cause I most often set in motion without realizing it is: ___________") is a sharp design choice.
