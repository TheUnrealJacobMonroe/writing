# PBI #204 — Topic Summary

## Proposed Title
**Neville Goddard's Secret: The Bible Is About You**

## Category
Manifestation & Creation (#5) — 3rd rotation

## The Hook
You were taught the Bible is about other people. About prophets who lived thousands of years ago. About a God who lives somewhere else and judges you from a distance. Neville Goddard spent his entire career arguing that this reading is backwards. The Bible is not history. It is not theology. It is a psychological manual, and every character, every event, every miracle is describing something that happens inside your own mind. This is not metaphor. This is the Hermetic Principle of Mentalism applied to scripture, and it changes everything about how you read the text.

## The Angle
This post takes three specific Bible stories and decodes them through Neville Goddard's lens, connecting each to a Hermetic principle.

**Genesis 1 (Creation):** God speaks and the world appears. Neville's reading: "God" is your own imagination. "Speaking" is the act of assuming something is true before evidence supports it. The seven days are not a timeline — they are the stages of mental creation. The Kybalion says the universe is mental. Genesis 1 is describing that principle in narrative form.

**The Prodigal Son:** A man leaves home, wastes everything, returns. Neville's reading: the "father" is your deeper self. The "far country" is the state of believing the external world is real and separate from you. The "return" is the moment you realize you never left — you were always creating your reality through assumption. The Principle of Correspondence: your inner state always matches your outer experience.

**The Resurrection:** Jesus dies and rises. Neville's reading: death is the moment you stop identifying with your current circumstances. Resurrection is the moment you assume a new state and begin to see it reflected in your world. This is Polarity in action — the shift from one extreme to another, not through force, but through assumption.

## What the Reader Walks Away With
A completely new way to read the Bible through Hermetic and Neville Goddard's framework. Three decoded stories that demonstrate the pattern: every Bible event is a psychological event happening in the reader's own mind.

## Why RIGHT NOW
Neville Goddard's work is experiencing a massive resurgence online, but most content cherry-picks quotes without showing the systematic framework. This post does the work of connecting specific stories to specific principles, giving readers both the "what" and the "why."
