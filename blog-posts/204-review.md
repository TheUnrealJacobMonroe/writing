## AI Pattern Flags (Advisory, Not Disqualifying)

**Negative parallelisms — Heavy use (10+ instances).**
"Not wrong exactly." "Not history. Not theology." "Not money. Not hedonism." "Not hidden... Not guarded." The post leans on this construction hard. In conversational Hermetic writing it reads as rhetorical rhythm rather than AI slop, but the density is notable. A lighter touch on 3–4 of these would make the ones that remain land harder.

**Rule of 3 — Used twice in near-identical form.**
"Three stories. Three decodings. Three Hermetic principles" (opening) mirrors "Three stories. Three principles. One mind. Your mind" (closing). One of these is a strong bookend. Both together feel slightly formulaic. Consider varying the closing.
